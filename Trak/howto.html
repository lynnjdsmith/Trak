How To
Enter triggers, symptoms and treatments in the log with as accurate timing as possible. Perfection is not required! Just try to be as complete as you can.
 
One Entry, Several Triggers
Two or more triggers can be in one entry. For example, 'Red Wine' is recorded as both red wine and alcohol.
 
Remember, the more accurately you record encounters with a particular trigger, the better we can predict if it's making you sick.
